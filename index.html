Our Natural Products

All Natural Products

One of the most common questions we get is about the cleaning products we use. It's straightforward - all 100% natural! We make every effort to be as environmentally friendly as possible. Dish soap, vinegar, hydrogen peroxide, essential oils, baking soda, and elbow grease are some of our key cleaning materials.

Universal Stone

Universal Stone is our go to multi-surface cleaner - Non-toxic, Acid-free, 100% Biodegradable. It is completely hypoallergenic too for any asthma sufferers and safe to use around pets and children. We love using this to clean, polish and protect your sinks, stove, appliances etc. We use this for all pour Deep Cleans which does the job without using those harmful chemical products.

Our FAQ's

What will it cost?

Based on the kind of cleaning you choose as well as the size and layout of your space, this will determine the cost of our visit. Your initial visit will be our Deep Clean service using our 101 point checklist. You may also "top-up" your booking as needed with our add-on services.

Who supplies the tools and cleaning products?

We provide the tools and cleaning products depending on your needs. All our Natural Clean Specialists are equipped with their own set of equipment such as a commercial vacuum cleaner and assorted brooms, mops, and biodegradable chemicals.

Are you environmentally conscious?

You betcha. We always strive to use environmentally friendly solutions and modify our cleaning processes to minimise waste whenever possible. Our cleaning kit contains eco-friendly materials, however our Natural Clean Specialists will never use bleach, or any harmful chemicals.

Are you fully insured?

We wouldn't be in business if we weren't! Natural Clean is covered by both Public and Employer's liability insurance. Furthermore, any accidental damages made by our Natural Clean Specialists working that are notified within 24 hours of the service date will be covered by the insurance. If requested, we may offer proof of insurance.

What if I'll need to cancel or reschedule my booking?

If you contact us 48 hours before our visit, we will gladly reschedule or cancel the booking. If a visit is cancelled with less than 48 hours' notice, we charge a 25% cancellation fee for the cost of the service.
